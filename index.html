Ping Pong

Here are the rules:

Ping Pong will count up to the number you enter with the exception of a few rules. What? You don't like rules? Well life is full of rules and they exist even on this web page. Sorry. So if you're ready, here they are:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "ping-pong"
